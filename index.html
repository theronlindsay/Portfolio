Upgrade Arena

Upgrade Arena is a Splitscreen Multiplayer Shooter built entirely by me using the Unity Game Engine. In Upgrade Arena, players control robots that get Upgraded when they lose to other players. Upgrades can add new abilities to the players gun, grant new movment abilities, or just boost stats like speed and damage. It gets chaotic quick!
